Our recent projects

Green Campus Project

Elohin University launched the Green Campus Project in 2020, with the aim of transforming its campus into one of the most sustainable in the country. The initiative includes the installation of solar panels on all buildings, the creation of community gardens and the implementation of a rainwater collection system. The project reduced the university's energy and water consumption by 40% in just two years.

Elohin HealthTech

In partnership with local hospitals and technology companies, Elohin University created Elohin HealthTech in 2021, an innovation center focused on developing health technologies. One of the main results was the development of an application that uses artificial intelligence to monitor patients' mental health, helping to accurately identify signs of depression and anxiety.
